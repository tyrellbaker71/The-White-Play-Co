Safety & Care

Play guidelines

01

Adult Supervision

Children must be supervised by a responsible adult at all times while using the equipment.

02

Shoes Off Please

Shoes must be removed before entering the play area — adults on mats too.

03

No Food or Drinks

To keep our luxury equipment pristine, food and drinks are not permitted on or around the play setup.

04

Protect Our Equipment

Face paint, slime, glitter, confetti, pens, and sharp objects are not permitted near the equipment.

05

No Pets

For hygiene and safety, pets are not allowed on or around the play equipment.

06

Home Hire Responsibility

Once installed, equipment remains the responsibility of the hirer until collection.
